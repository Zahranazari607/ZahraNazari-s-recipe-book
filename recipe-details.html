Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix well.
  4. Bake for 30–35 minutes.

Chicken Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • 2 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion

Instructions

  1. Grill the chicken with olive oil and seasoning.
  2. Toast the buns lightly.
  3. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, onion, and chicken.

Vegan Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 avocado
  • Lemon juice, olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa and let it cool.
  2. Mix with chopped vegetables and avocado.
  3. Add lemon juice and olive oil to taste.
